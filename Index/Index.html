Mickaël Wahbi

Welcome to my page

This page is all over my life where I will
write everything happened to me when
I fled as an immigrant from my country(Syria)
to Europe(Belgium).

The Begin:

It all started in Syria, when
ISIS came to Syria and destroyed
everything and of course Bashar
Al Assad killed people as
well. In the beginning, it was all a little bit
calmer but there were protests with the president and
against him, as well as kidnappings for
ransom. People would block the streets so that
no one enters or exits it, they had very melee weapons
like knifes, sticks and rocks.

One time, two RPG-7 hit our town one next to the house
of my grandmother; where I was as well,
the windows, the doors everything was shaking,
the horns of the cars were so loud.
The first rocket hit a house 50 meters next to us.
Me and my dad went to see what happened
and all other men and women of the street.
The second one hit the roof of
a house in the next neighborhood. Thanks god none of them exploded.

The next couple of days went good until
one day at night we hard weapons
next to our house and at that moment
our neighbor was at our house.
and his house was really close to our house
but he couldn't go because some of ISIS men
were literally beside it, like there was
only one wall between us. We could hear everything they said.
We had to turn all lights off and be quite.

The next day my dad decided to go and live with
my grandmother for a while then my dad
said it would be more safer if we went
somewhere else like Lebanon. So my dad
had to call to my school to tell them
that I will travel and if they let me pass the
year without doing my exams. My principle
said "no, that's not possible", so
I had to leave my school, I was at that moment like 9
about to be 10 years old. My dad had to quit his
job so he can pass the borders, because they wouldn't
let anyone pass that works for the government
and by that I don't mean like a police agent
or someone like that. My dad used to be
a train driver. What made him choose this
choice is because he couldn't go to his job anymore
because like I said in the beginning of my story,
that people of other towns and villages would
block the streets and there were no other ways
to go there and they said that if they see my dad again they will kill him.

So after that we called a taxi to come pick us
up 2 days later to go to Lebanon, where we can
find a better life. When I arrived to the place in Lebanon
I looked around me and saw all this beauty
I was happy. I turned around and saw a big monastery we entered
there the priesters welcomed us joyfully and
we started get to know each other.
